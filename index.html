Ical Integration

It is possible to use every ical compatible calendar as a source for tasks. This way you cal-done is allways aware of all your events and schedules tasks accordingly. You can also manipulate these calendars from within the app if those calendars support it. This way you can integrate for example apple icloud and microsoft outlook
